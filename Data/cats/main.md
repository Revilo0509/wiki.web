# Cats

## Overview

Cats Felis catus are small domesticated carnivorous mammals that
have lived alongside humans for thousands of years. They are known for
their agility, independence, and affectionate companionship.

## Characteristics

-   Belong to the family Felidae
-   Known for grace, sharp reflexes, and hunting skills
-   Highly adaptable to different environments
-   Communicate through vocalizations, body language, and purring

## Roles in Human Society

-   Kept as pets for companionship
-   Natural pest control by hunting rodents
-   Appear in cultural symbols, folklore, and mythology
-   Emotional support and therapy animals

## Breeds

Some popular cat breeds include: - Persian - Maine Coon - Siamese -
Bengal - Ragdoll - Sphynx

## Lifespan and Care

-   Average lifespan: 12--18 years depending on breed and care
-   Require a balanced diet, regular veterinary care, and mental
    stimulation
-   Enjoy climbing, scratching, and interactive play
-   Can thrive both indoors and outdoors, though indoor cats live longer

## Fun Facts

-   Cats can rotate their ears 180 degrees
-   Domestic cats are believed to have been domesticated around 9,000
    years ago
-   A group of cats is called a "clowder"
-   The oldest known pet cat was found in a 9,500-year-old grave on
    Cyprus
