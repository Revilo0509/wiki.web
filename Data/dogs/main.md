# Dogs

## Overview

Dogs, Canis lupus familiaris, are domesticated mammals, not natural
wild animals. They were originally bred from wolves and have been
companions to humans for thousands of years.

## Characteristics

-   Belong to the family Canidae
-   Known for loyalty and companionship
-   Have a wide variety of breeds with different sizes, shapes, and
    temperaments
-   Possess an acute sense of smell and hearing

## Roles in Human Society

-   Companionship as pets
-   Working dogs (herding, hunting, guarding)
-   Service animals (guide dogs, therapy dogs, rescue dogs)
-   Detection (police dogs, military dogs, drug and explosive detection)

## Breeds

Some popular dog breeds include: - Labrador Retriever - German
Shepherd - Golden Retriever - Bulldog - Poodle - Beagle

## Lifespan and Care

-   Average lifespan: 10--15 years depending on breed and size
-   Require proper diet, exercise, grooming, and regular veterinary
    checkups
-   Social animals that thrive with interaction and attention

## Fun Facts

-   Dogs can understand human emotions and commands
-   They have been domesticated for over 15,000 years
-   The Basenji breed is known as the "barkless dog" because it makes
    unique vocal sounds instead of barking
