# Music

## Overview

Music is the art of arranging sounds in time to create rhythm, melody, and harmony.  
It has been a core part of human culture for thousands of years, used for expression, storytelling, and social connection.

## Characteristics

- Universal form of human expression
- Combines rhythm, melody, harmony, and dynamics
- Found in every culture and society
- Can influence emotions and moods

## Roles in Human Society

- Entertainment and artistic expression
- Rituals, ceremonies, and religious practices
- Cultural identity and tradition
- Therapy and mental health support

## Genres

Some popular music genres include:  
- Classical  
- Jazz  
- Rock  
- Hip-hop  
- Pop  
- Electronic  

## Lifespan and Care

- Instruments require maintenance and tuning
- Musicians need regular practice and training
- Listening habits evolve across generations
- Recorded music preserves works for centuries
